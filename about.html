A LITTLE ABOUT ME...

Hi, my name is Darsh, I am a Front End Web Developer based in London, United Kingdom.

I have a strong passion for building awesome looking websites that are fully functional, responsive and professional whilst using best practices and the latest W3C standards guidelines. All of my websites are lovingly hand coded.


I consider design to be just as important as development and aim to combine both to produce high quality user experience.

For a little over a year to the present I have been educating myself HTML 5, CSS 3, JavaScript, JQuery and responsive design, soon to be followed by PHP, MySQL, Wordpress, AJAX, JSON, Git, GitHub and Python. For Building my websites I use Sublime Text and Photoshop.


I Graduated from Leicester De Montfort University in 2009 with a BSc (Hons) in Media Technology.

I am open to exciting new career opportunities, with my Curriculum Vitae available on request. Please get in touch, it would be great to hear from you.
